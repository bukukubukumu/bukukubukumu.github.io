About Us

We are BukukuBukumu!

Bukuku Bukumu literally means "My Book, Your Book" in Bahasa Indonesia. Someone’s used book can still be someone else’s new book. While the value of a used book might diminish to the owner, the very same book promises new opportunities to a different reader. We wish to take advantage of this and distribute used books to those who have not had a chance to learn from them. Our vision is that kids in Indonesia, wherever they are, will have access to a collection of books that will inspire them to learn and to grow.

Our mission is to empower all of Indonesia’s children in need by providing them with. Being such a vast country, this poses a lot of challenges. However, with the support of our volunteers and partners, we believe we can help make a change.

As we are very early on in our journey, we are trying multiple pilot programs to accomplish our mission and vision. Stay tuned to see our newest programs and latest developments!
